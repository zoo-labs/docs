# Welcome to Zoo Docs

Zoo Labs Foundation (501c3) is building decentralized infrastructure for AI training and collective intelligence.

## Mission

Democratizing AI through:

- **Decentralized AI Training**: Distributed compute marketplace
- **Open Source Frontier AI**: Zen model family (600M-480B parameters)
- **Collective Intelligence**: Byzantine-robust coordination (DSO)
- **Verifiable AI Compute**: Proof of AI (PoAI) consensus
- **Fair Distribution**: October 2021 genesis (100% airdrop to CryptoZoo)

## Core Protocols

### DSO - Decentralized Semantic Optimization

Multi-agent coordination protocol for distributed learning. Built on Hanzo's Active Semantic Optimization (ASO).

[Read the DSO Paper →](https://zooai.github.io/papers/zoo-dso.pdf)

### PoAI - Proof of AI

Consensus mechanism for verifiable AI compute with TEE attestations and quality verification.

[Read ZIP-002 →](https://github.com/zooai/ZIPs/blob/main/ZIP-002-proof-of-ai.md)
